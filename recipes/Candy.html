Candy

This is a picture of my nephews' go-to recipe for sugar cravings

Yes, they are children and they need to be careful not to eat too much sugar !

Here are some of the needed ingredients

  • sugar
  • water
  • food coloring
  • corn syrup

How to prepare candy !

  1. Grease a cookie sheet
  2. Stir white sugar, corn syrup, and water
  3. Heat, stir, let cool until hardened and enjoy !
